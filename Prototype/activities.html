Activities

Volcano Trail

We offer guided tours up to our local volcano. Our volcano has been designated dormant and allows for trips to the summit. there are several different difficulty levels to choose from.

Fishing Charters

Check out our fishing tours. Fishing is a staple in the Taniti diet. There are many unique fish in Taniti. Fish is a major part of the Taniti diet.

Jungle Hikes

Check out the vast jungle and experience the unique native species of birds, reptiles, and mammals. Always remember to plan appopraitely and bring enough water to stay hydrated. There are many rivers that flow through the Taniti jungle.
