My second project utilizing JavaFX and Scene Builder


Development Journal for Scheduling App

This application is designed for managing customers and appointments and provide reports on data collected from a mySQL database. Appointments and customers will be tracked by a unique primary key tracked by the DBMS.


How data will be displayed

Data will be displayed using table views to show customer and appointment information. Labels will be used for some warning messaging and dialog boxes will be used to handle other errors and confirmations.


Controls used for application

Buttons, Combo boxes,date pickers, and textFields will be the primary controls used for adding and modifying customer and appointment information.
