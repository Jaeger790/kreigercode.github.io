About Me

I am a software developer. I graduated from Western Governors University this year and I am excited to start career as a developer. I enjoy learning new technologies and languages and I am constantly working to improve my skill-sets in programming and user experience. My language skills consist of Java, C#, C++, python, and javascript. I am interested in machine learning, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and game development. I have a portfolio at Github Feel free to check it out.

After 2020 I decided to switch careers and pursue my degree in computer science. I have always had a passion for technology and proclivity to reverse engineer everything I could get my hands on. My career before in construction gave me an understanding of project management, working with small and large teams to accomplish common goals, and problem solving skills to fix various problems like: broken equipment, scheduling conflicts, negotiations purchasing contracts, and handling project realized project risks. Being able to incorporate these broad scope skills have allowed me to transition into IT and finish school 1.5 years early. The problem solving skills I learned from fixing equipment has a lot of overlap with debugging. Starting with the most simple and obvious potential cause for a problem and working towards more complicated solutions.
