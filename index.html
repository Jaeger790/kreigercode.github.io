About Me

I am a software engineering student and I have 15 years experience with reparing, building, and troubleshooting Windows, Mac, and Linux systems.

I got my start in computers very early in life playing and creating custom StarCraft campaigns and games. The way computers allowed me to connect with my friends at school and the games we could play together when we could not be in person forever changed my perspective on what was possible. Today these revolutionary ideas are accepted as standard practice, but the wonder I have towards developing new ideas using ever evolving technology has never dulled.
