About Me

I am a software developer currently attending WGU. I anticipate graduating with a Bachelors in March of 2023. I enjoy learning new technologies and languages and I am constantly working to improve my skill-sets in programming and user experience. Currently my best language is Java. I am currently working on learning C# and the .NET framework as well. I am always open to new project ideas and enjoy working in an agile environment. I am also interested in web development, game development and machine learning.

When I am not programming I enjoy spending time outdoors. I have been camping all throughout the western U.S. and I wish to see more. My dog Freya enjoys going on hikes with me and has even guided me back to my truck when I got lost on a trail once. I am always working on new skills and I enjoy the satisfaction that DIY brings. I got a certification for HVAC after my A/C broke. I value self reliance but understand that it is important to ask for help when progress has been halted. I play video games occasionally and generally enjoy RPG and strategy games.
