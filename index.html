About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website!

I am Brad Rott, and I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Software Development from Western Governors University. I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented software developer with a passion for challenge and excitement.

Throughout my education, I gained proficiency in a range of programming languages, including Java, Python, and C++, as well as experience in software development methodologies, such as Agile and Scrum, and using development tools such as Git.

Currently I am working on my developer certification as a continuation of my education.

In addition to my software development experience, I also have experience in IT and procurement, where I developed skills in troubleshooting, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and please feel free to explore my projects and contact me with any questions or opportunities.

Inventory Management

This application utilizes Java 19 and JavaFX. The purpose of the application is to demonstrate class abstraction, polymorphism, encapsulation, and inheritance. The application allows for users to enter product information and save the details to a list that can be viewed from the home-screen. The application also includes a system for tracking parts and allows the ability to associate parts to a particular product.
